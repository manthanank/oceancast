Tide Science Guideline

Semi-Diurnal Cycle

Most coastal locations experience a semi-diurnal tide cycle, consisting of two high tides and two low tides every 24 hours and 50 minutes.

Surfing Impact

Many beach breaks are tide-sensitive. High tides can swallow wave energy creating "fat" waves, while very low tides can make waves blocky or trigger quick closeouts.

Fishing Impact

Fish feed heavily during tidal flow changes (rising or falling water levels), as currents move baitfish. High tide turns are traditionally very productive.
